# **Wilcoxon Signed-Rank Test**

The [[Wilcoxon signed-rank test]] is a [[non-parametric test]] that compares two related measurements, serving as the non-parametric alternative to the [[paired t-test]].

## **When to Use It**

* Paired measurements where the differences are not normally distributed.
* Ordinal or heavily skewed paired data.

## **Interpreting the Results**

* The test ranks the absolute differences between paired observations and compares positive vs. negative ranks.
* A significant [[p-value]] indicates a systematic shift between the two conditions.
