# **Paired T-Test**

The [[paired t-test]] compares two related measurements from the **same subjects** — for example, before and after treatment.

## **How to Use**

1. Go to **Analysis > Tests > Paired T-Test**.
2. Select the two columns representing the paired measurements.

## **Assumptions**

* The differences between paired measurements are approximately [[normality|normally distributed]].
* Each pair comes from the same subject or matched unit.

## **Interpreting the Results**

* The test focuses on the **mean difference** between the two measurements.
* A significant [[p-value]] indicates that the mean change is unlikely to be zero.

## **Tips**

* If the differences are not normally distributed, use the [[Wilcoxon signed-rank test]] instead.
