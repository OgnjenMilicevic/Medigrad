# **Mann-Whitney U Test**

The [[Mann-Whitney U test]] is a [[non-parametric test]] that compares the distributions of two **independent** groups without assuming normality.

## **When to Use It**

* The data are not normally distributed.
* You have ordinal data or heavily skewed distributions.
* It serves as the non-parametric alternative to the [[independent t-test]].

## **Interpreting the Results**

* The test statistic (U) is based on the ranks of all observations pooled together.
* A significant [[p-value]] means one group's values tend to be systematically higher or lower than the other's.
* Pair with the [[rank-biserial correlation]] as an [[effect size]] measure.
